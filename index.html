Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

This framework highlights the critical areas for innovation and growth in shaping Bangladesh 2.0.

Sustainability

By embedding sustainability across all pillars, Bangladesh 2.0 aims to balance innovation and progress with ecological integrity and societal equity.

Infrastructure

By prioritizing modern, sustainable, and resilient infrastructure, Bangladesh 2.0 aims to accelerate development and improve the quality of life for its citizens while fostering innovation and global integration

Empowerment

Through a holistic approach to empowerment, Bangladesh 2.0 envisions an inclusive society where every individual, regardless of gender, age, or background, has the opportunity to thrive and contribute to national progress.

The Vision of Future

Bangladesh 2.0 aims to redefine the nation’s trajectory by embracing cutting-edge technology, promoting inclusive growth, and ensuring sustainability. With initiatives in renewable energy, digital transformation, and robust infrastructure, the country is set to become a global model of development.

Digital Transformation

Creating a nationwide digital ecosystem to enhance connectivity and access to services for all citizens.

Green Energy

Investments in solar and wind energy to ensure a sustainable and eco-friendly future.

Modern Infrastructure

Building state-of-the-art infrastructure to support economic growth and global competitiveness.
